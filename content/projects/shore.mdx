Shore is my answer to the question: what if static site generators optimized for reading instead of features?

## Design Principles

- **No JavaScript by default.** Your blog doesn't need a framework.
- **Typography first.** Beautiful reading experience without configuration.
- **Fast builds.** Rebuilds in milliseconds, not seconds.

## Current Status

Used in production for several personal blogs. Still evolving based on real-world usage.
