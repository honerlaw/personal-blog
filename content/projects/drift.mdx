Drift is an experiment in local-first software. It works offline, syncs instantly when connected, and never loses your edits—even when multiple people edit the same document simultaneously.

## Technical Approach

Built on Yjs for CRDT operations, with a custom WebSocket server for real-time sync. The editor uses CodeMirror 6 for a native-feeling editing experience.

## Why I Built This

I wanted to understand CRDTs beyond the theory. Building Drift taught me that the hard part isn't the algorithm—it's the UX around conflict resolution and presence awareness.
