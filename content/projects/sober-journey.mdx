Track your sobriety, your way.

A gentle tool to help you notice time since your last action — substances, behaviors, or patterns. The app adapts to whatever you're working on, at your own pace.

## What It Does

- **Track any habit.** Whatever you're working on, SoberJourney adapts to your journey.
- **See your progress.** Watch time accumulate since your last reset. Simple, clear, and always there when you need it.
- **Reset when needed.** Life happens. Reset your timer without shame and start fresh — every moment is a new beginning.

Live at [soberjourney.app](https://www.soberjourney.app/).
